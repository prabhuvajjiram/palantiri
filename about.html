Our Mission

At Palantiri, we exist to provide seamless technical operations and backend support services exclusively for Angel Stones, enabling them to focus on their core monument business in the United States while we handle their technical operations from India.

Our mission is to serve as an integral extension of Angel Stones' team, delivering technical expertise, operational efficiency, and consistent quality that drives their business forward across time zones.

Deliver reliable technical operations support to streamline Angel Stones' business processes

Provide expert CAD design services for monument customization with quick turnaround times

Develop and maintain specialized software solutions tailored to the monument industry

Our Journey

The story of Palantiri's growth as Angel Stones' technical operations partner

2024

Beginning of Operations

Palantiri was established in 2024 as the dedicated technical operations partner for Angel Stones, a leading monument wholesaler in the United States. We started with a focused team providing backend operations support and CAD design services.

2025

Expanded Service Offerings

We achieved significant growth in our first year, expanding our services to include custom software development solutions specifically tailored for the monument industry. This allowed Angel Stones to streamline their operations and enhance customer service.
