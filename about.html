Our Story

Palantiri Technology and Services was founded with a clear vision: to provide exceptional technology solutions and backend operational support that enables businesses to thrive in the digital era.

Based in India, we started as the dedicated technology partner for Angel Stones LLC, delivering comprehensive backend operations, software development, and technical support services. Our success in this partnership demonstrated our ability to seamlessly integrate with organizations across continents.

Today, while maintaining our close partnership with Angel Stones, we're expanding our horizons to collaborate with businesses across industries who share our commitment to quality, innovation, and excellence.

Our Leadership

Founded by experienced technology professionals with backgrounds in software development, database management, and digital operations, Palantiri brings together expertise from various domains to create comprehensive solutions for our clients.

Our leadership team combines technical knowledge with a deep understanding of business processes, allowing us to deliver services that not only meet technical requirements but also address real business challenges.
